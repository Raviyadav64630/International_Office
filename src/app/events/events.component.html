"Students: inside talk" online seminar Day : 25 January 2022

The application phase for the 2022 winter semester begins on April 15, 2022. The Central Student Advisory Service at Deggendorf University of Technology provides information on the topics of application, deadlines and admissions and offers general information on the courses at the university. The online seminar "Students: Inside Talk" will take place on Wednesday, January 25 from 4 to 5 p.m. All interested parties are invited, including parents and teachers who want to find out more about THD. Contact: zsb@th-deg.de
